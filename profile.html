Body fat percentage is the total mass of fat divided by total
body mass. The typical bodyfat percentage is 18-24% for an
average male and 25-31% for an average female according to wikipedia. If you don't know yours it's not a big deal - we only use it to estimate your lean body mass and change the minimum amount of protein recommended in the nutrition calculator.
